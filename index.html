About us

Hello World Hub is a community provided for developers to learn and to interact, share ideas and solutions, network with each other.

Mission

To help people who have genuine interest and passion for technology to interact with each other and we also equip them with relevant information and materials that will help them in their learning process, thus equiping them with adequate skill set and hopefully gain employment.
